SCUDERIA FERRARI
The BEAST

Ferrari's Formula One (F1) cars are the pinnacle of automotive engineering and performance. They are highly advanced, aerodynamically designed machines that are capable of achieving incredible speeds and handling with precision and agility. Ferrari F1 cars are powered by turbocharged V6 engines that produce around 700 horsepower, and they feature advanced electronic systems that control everything from engine performance to suspension and braking. The team's iconic red livery and prancing horse emblem are instantly recognizable around the world, and their passionate fans are some of the most dedicated in all of motorsport. Ferrari F1 cars have won numerous championships over the years and remain a dominant force in the sport to this day.

Scuderia Ferrari

The Ferrari F1 team is renowned for its passion, dedication, and tireless work ethic. From the mechanics to the engineers, every member of the team plays a vital role in the success of the Ferrari brand on the racetrack. Their ability to work together seamlessly and overcome challenges is truly inspiring. The hard work and dedication of the Ferrari F1 work team is a testament to what can be achieved through perseverance and teamwork. We salute them for their incredible achievements and look forward to their continued success on the track.

MEET OUR DRIVERS

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc is a Monegasque racing driver currently competing in Formula One for Scuderia Ferrari. He is a former Formula Two champion and has earned multiple podium finishes and pole positions in Formula One. Leclerc is known for his speed, racecraft, and precision driving, and is considered one of the most promising young drivers in the sport.

Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz is a Spanish racing driver who competes in Formula One for Ferrari. He was born on April 12, 1994, in Madrid, Spain. Sainz made his Formula One debut in 2015 with Toro Rosso and has since driven for Renault, McLaren, and now Ferrari. He has earned a reputation as a consistent and versatile driver, known for his speed and racecraft
